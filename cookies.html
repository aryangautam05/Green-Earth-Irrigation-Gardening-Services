Cookies Policy

Effective Date: June 2024

This Cookies Policy explains how Green Earth Irrigation & Gardening Services ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website and interact with our services. Your privacy and trust are very important to us, and we want you to understand how cookies function on our site and how you can manage your cookie preferences.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) by websites you visit. They help websites remember your preferences, enable core functionality, gather analytics, and improve the browsing experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use several categories of cookies to provide, maintain, and enhance your experience on our site:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for basic site functions, such as page navigation and security features. Without these, the website may not operate effectively.
  • Performance and Analytics Cookies: These cookies collect anonymized information about how visitors use our website, such as pages visited, time spent, and errors encountered. This data helps us improve the website and our services.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies remember your preferences, like language settings or form inputs, to personalize your experience.
  • Advertising and Tracking Cookies: We do not use third-party advertising cookies on our website. We respect your privacy and do not serve targeted ads.

3. How We Use Cookies

Cookies help us to:

  • Remember your privacy and cookie preferences to avoid asking repeatedly.
  • Maintain your login sessions securely if applicable.
  • Analyze website performance and usage trends to improve content and service delivery.
  • Ensure security measures work effectively.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We use trusted third-party providers for analytics, like Google Analytics, which may place cookies on your device. These cookies collect information about your website usage but do not identify you personally. For more details, please refer to their privacy policies.

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have the right to decide whether to accept or decline cookies. You can adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies; however, some features of our website may not function properly if you disable cookies.

Additionally, the cookie consent dialog at the bottom of the page allows you to accept or decline non-essential cookies directly. Your choice will be remembered for future visits.

6. What Happens if You Decline Cookies?

If you decline non-essential cookies, you will still be able to use most parts of our website, but some features that rely on those cookies may be limited or unavailable. Essential cookies will still be used to ensure basic functionality and security.

7. Updating This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time to reflect changes to our practices or legal requirements. We encourage you to check this page periodically to stay informed.
